Fran Lusty

Fran Lusty is a 6 foot singer-songwriter based in London with Norwegian roots. Her journey into the world of indie-folk has been marked by a commitment to storytelling through music. She enjoys writing about the enchantment of the natural world and the melancholy of its struggles.

Her tracks have been described as dreamful and truly transformative, perfect for intimate venues where every note carries authenticity. Fran has performed all across London, establishing a strong presence in the city's live music scene. She regularly plays Sofar Sounds and is a resident artist at the Troubadour in Earl's Court after selling out two headline shows in 2023.

Fran released her debut single in January 2021. It was mostly for her friends to listen to but quickly gained traction from a wider audience. The singer-songwriter has received ongoing support from BBC Introducing (including Track of the Week with 'Oceans Apart'), Amazing Radio and Future Hits.

Inspired by Damien Rice and Virginia Woolf's storytelling, Fran Lusty is set to release her debut EP on the 22nd September 2023.

Fun fact: Fran opened up for LP at a mutual friend's birthday party.
